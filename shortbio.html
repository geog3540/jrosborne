Joshie Osborne University of Iowa Student

I am currently a student at the Univsersity of Iowa studying the environmental sciences, sustainability, geoscience, and the geographical information sciences. My passion lies within the veil of the United States' forests, in the nature of wildfire and wildfire ecology. I aim to attend graduate school to recieve a masters in fire ecology and management, utilizing my knowledge of geospatial data analytics, GIS softwares, remote sensing techniques, and geovisualization. I have experience working with wildfire firsthand on a type-2 initial attack engine crew in the Kootenai National Forest in Montana's northwestern corner. This experience has taught me the extreme importance of GIS in the world of wildfire for precise planning and disaster remediation. Through the knowledge I have gained in creating this portfolio, I will be able to effectively contribute to making our forests more safe.
